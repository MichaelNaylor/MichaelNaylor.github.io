Computer Systems Fundamentals Unit


About this unit


For the first term of the Computer Systems Fundametals unit we have 2 lectures each week. 1 lecture focusses on maths and one on computer logic and assembly language. For the first 7 weeks we are focussing on logic gates and boolean algebra to understand how the ALU works in modern computer systems before moving on to using Mars MIPS to create programs in assembly language. To construct logic gates we are using a piece of software called Logisim


This has so far been the most challenging unit for me personally as it focusses on concepts that I have not studied before like boolean algebra and digital logic.
