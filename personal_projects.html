This is the biggest of my current personal projects. I am trying to recreate the popular incremental game Clicker Heroes in HTML5 and Javascript. I chose this game as it has a lot of different mechaning for a game in the incremental genre and it requires me to find ways to deal with exponentially increasing numbers.
